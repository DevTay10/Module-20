bloom is a plant app based around community, trading, and care tips and tasks, that is an all-in-one solution for your plant needs.

Plant lovers we interviewed were less likely to get involved in an in-person plant community but still wanted to share their plants online
We also had users tell us they would like reminders to do tasks such as watering, pruning, an repotting
Users wanted an easier way to find plant care information specifically catered to plants they owned

We wanted to build an app that would be a one-stop-shop for information, plant care reminders, and be a community for plant lovers to share pictures and pro-tips about their plants

In a team of 4, I focused on the development of the User Flows and the wireframing of the Forums section. The rest of the process was done collaboratively.
